Big Mac

Description:

A Big Mac is a Maccas Classic.
Double everything?! Accompanied by The BEST Burger Sauce.
So Yum!
Originally Being an Occasional Treat, Can Now be Created Within Your own Kitchen.
HUGE Thank You to Big Mac Burger Sauce!

Ingredients:

  • The finest Sesame Tri-Buns
  • The finest 100% Beef Patties
  • The finest NZ grown Fresh Lettuce
  • The finest finely Diced Onions
  • The finest Cheddar Cheese
  • Big Mac Burger Sauce
  • Sliced Pickles

Steps:

  1. Bottom Sesame Bun, steamed or toasted
  2. Big Mac Sauce
  3. Shredded Lettuce
  4. Diced Onion
  5. Grilled Patty and Cheese
  6. Middle/Bottom Bun, steamed or toasted
  7. Big Mac Sauce
  8. Shredded Lettuce
  9. Diced Onion
  10. Pickles
  11. Grilled Patty
  12. Top Bun, steamed or toasted
