Quarter Pounder

Description:

A Quarter Pounder is a Maccas Classic.
Quarter Pound of Beef?! Accompanied by The Classic Deliciousness of The Cheese Burger.
Oh My?!
Originally Being an Occasional Treat, Can Now be Created Within Your own Kitchen.
HUGE Thank You to The Quarter Pounder Patties!

Ingredients:

  • The finest Sesame Buns
  • The finest 100% Beef QP Patties
  • The finest finely Diced Onions
  • The finest Cheddar Cheese
  • Ketchup
  • Mustard
  • Sliced Pickles

Steps:

  1. Bottom Bun, steamed or toasted
  2. Cheese
  3. Grilled QP Patty
  4. Cheese
  5. Sliced Pickles
  6. Onions
  7. Mustard
  8. Ketchup
  9. Top Sesame Bun, steamed or toasted
